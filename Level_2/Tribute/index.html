Jerry John Rawlings

Former President of Ghana

Biography

Jerry John Rawlings was born in Accra, Gold Coast, on 22 June 1947, to Victoria Agbotui (n√©e Agbenyegah) and James Ramsey John, a chemist from Castle Douglas in Kirkcudbrightshire, Scotland. Rawlings attended Achimota School and a military academy at Teshie. In 1968, he earned the rank of Flight Lieutenant in the Ghanaian Air Force following his graduation from the Ghana Military Academy and the Royal Military Academy Sandhurst, where he was described as "the most outstanding cadet" of his intake. He saw service during the Nigerian Civil War, which he discussed in an interview with the BBC.
